How we work

A family business, since 1994.

Founded in 1994 and run by our family ever since — three decades of relationships across mills and finishers in Korea and Asia, so you get the right hand-feel, color and lead time without managing the supply chain yourself.

01Curated sourcing

We match your design intent to the right base — drape, weight, finish — across a vetted network built since 1994, with sourcing reach through Asia.

02Sampling on request

Counter samples, S/O and lab dips to your standard, so you can confirm color and quality before committing to bulk.

03Stable supply

Consistent lots and color management across repeat orders, backed by long-standing relationships built over thirty years.
